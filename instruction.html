How To Play: Follow the instructions:

PUZZLE GAME

You must first choose which picture you wish to use. A difficulty selection screen
will appear. You Then choose the difficulty level. You will then be presented
with a scrambled version of the picture you chose. The harder the difficulty,
the more squares the picture will be divided into.

The GOAL is to reassemble the puzzle pieces. Click on the first pieces. Click on
the first piece that you want to swap. The color surrounding the piece will change
to blue. Click on the piece you want to swap it with. The piece then will be swapped.
